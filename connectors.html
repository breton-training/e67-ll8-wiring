Build Notes & Anomalies

Do not resolve any of the items below by assumption. Mark as Suspect until confirmed by GM SI, continuity testing, or electrical behavior measurement.
  • Two pins both labeled "Starter Enable Relay Control": X1-56 (YE/BK, 625) and X2-43 (YE, 6259, 4.2L). Suspect / conflict Verify primary against factory wiring diagram before final connection.
  • Two pins both labeled "IC 4 Control" with circuit 2124: X2-18 (D-GN/WH, 0.5mm²) and X2-34 (D-GN/WH, 0.35mm²). Same circuit on two pins is unusual. Suspect / conflict Verify before wiring coils.
  • Any pin/function conflict between CSV, SI, and physical harness should be flagged as Suspect and resolved by physical continuity or scope measurement before final connection.
  • Wire gauge guide: 0.35mm² ≈ 22 AWG, 0.5mm² ≈ 20 AWG, 1.0mm² ≈ 18 AWG (primary ground X2-73 is heavy gauge).
  • Color codes: BK=Black, BN=Brown, RD=Red, OG=Orange, YE=Yellow, GN=Green, BU=Blue, PU=Purple, GY=Gray, WH=White, PK=Pink, TN=Tan. Prefix D-=Dark, L-=Light.
  • Two-tone wire format: BASE/TRACER. The first color is the insulation jacket, the second is the stripe.
  • 4.2L tag indicates pin function is specific to the Atlas I6 calibration. Same physical pin position may have different function on 5.3L variant.
  • For bench flash minimum: power = X1-20 (B+); ignition = X1-18, X1-19, X2-13 tied; ground = X2-73 + OBDII pins 4/5; CAN = X3-33 (low) + X3-53 (high). Sensor low references are not primary grounds and are not required for bench flash.
  • X1 layout + position + wire color physically verified 2026-04-28. X2/X3 connector layouts verified, but individual circuit population, color, and function verification is In progress as of 2026-05-09. X1: 4 cols × 14 rows uniform. X2/X3: 2+2 column structure — short side (left) 2 columns of 16 pins, long side (right) 2 columns of 20 pins, plus pin 73 in special larger position above short side.
